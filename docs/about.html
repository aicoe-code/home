Purpose

The AICOE site helps teams understand, design, build, deploy, and operate AI solutions safely and effectively. It curates opinionated guidance, reusable patterns, and reference assets that reduce time‑to‑value while promoting good engineering and governance.

Audience

  • Practitioners: data scientists, ML engineers, software and platform engineers
  • Leads: product, delivery, and technical leaders shaping strategy and roadmaps
  • Partners: security, risk/compliance, and architecture review teams

Writing Guidelines

  • Be concise and practical; lead with the simplest working path.
  • Structure pages with clear headings; add alt text and maintain good contrast.
  • Include prerequisites, exact steps, expected results, and cleanup/rollback.
  • Do not include secrets; use environment variables or a secrets manager.

Contribute

Open a PR or push small, focused commits to main. For substantive guidance, request a second reviewer (content + technical). Prefer canonical sources and note version/date where relevant.
